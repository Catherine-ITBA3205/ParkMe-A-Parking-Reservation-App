Help How can we help you?
 How to start using the application?
You will be prompted with a login and sign up screen. In order to access the app, you need to create/login an account.

How to start looking for Parking Spaces?
Click the "Book Your Slot" icon, a map or list of parking spaces will be displayed. And once you have selected your desired booking you will be required to enter information such as your vehicle plate number, parking time and phone number. And there will be an automatic slot id provided for you.

Do you offer overnight parking?
It depends on the parking space! If there are parking spaces in Batangas City that offers overnight parking, then you will be able to to see it. Just adjust the time frame for your desired parking time.

Does every place offer 24/7 access to my car?
Many parking spaces in Batangas City are usually only open during mall hours, as they are assigned as a mall parking space. If limited opertaing hours arre in place at ay given location. It will be noted under the "details/description" section of your desired parking space, please read it before confirming your booking.
